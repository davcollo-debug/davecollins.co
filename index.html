About Dave

Dave is a product executive with 20 years of experience across Australian and international tech companies.

Most recently Dave was Chief Product Officer at Hireup, Australia's largest NDIS marketplace. He led product, design, and marketing through significant growth — scaling the disability business and expanding into aged care, whilst navigating the NDIS regulatory environment.

Before that Dave was Head of Product at ImmoScout24, Germany's leading property platform, based in Berlin. He led product, design, research, and marketing for the New Homes business during a period of double-digit growth. Dave also drove the rapid growth of ImmoScout24's mobile apps to market dominance across Germany.

Earlier in his career Dave led mobile product at REA Group during a fiercely competitive period with Domain. He started at Sensis as online directories were being dismantled by Google Maps — an early lesson in what happens when innovation disrupts markets.

Dave works primarily with mature and scale-up product companies in regulated industries, be it PropTech, HealthTech, Vertical SaaS and Marketplaces. He is based in Melbourne, and is available across Australia and remotely worldwide.
