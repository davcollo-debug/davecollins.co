Services

How I can help

Product Assessment & Diligence

Need your product team to move from good to excellent? I'll assess your product strategy, vision, org structure, and discovery and delivery capabilities across product, design, and engineering. You'll know which features to ship and which ones to kill.

I can also run a growth assessment of your web or mobile product, evaluating your ability to sustainably acquire, retain, and monetise customers.

Buying or selling? I conduct product and team diligence so you know exactly what you're getting.

Coaching & Advisory

If you're a CEO or CPO looking to sharpen your product leadership, I offer tailored programmes covering product vision, strategy, and the kind of inspiring leadership that actually engages teams.

For teams needing to level up their discovery and delivery capabilities, I'll roll up my sleeves and work through the real challenges with you.

Interim / Fractional Product Leadership

Got an urgent leadership gap? I can step in at CPO or Head of Product level and get things moving while you find the right permanent hire.
