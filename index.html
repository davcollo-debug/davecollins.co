About Dave

I'm a product leader with 20 years of experience building and fixing product organisations across regulated industries, two-sided marketplaces, and international scale-ups. I've spent my career in the seat — not advising from a distance — which is what makes the counsel useful.

Most recently I was Chief Product Officer at Hireup, Australia's largest NDIS marketplace, where I was brought in to transform the product organisation and scale across regulated markets. Over four and a half years I led product, design, marketing, and engineering through significant growth — expanding from disability services into aged care — whilst navigating the complexity of NDIS regulation. I left with a high-performing, self-sustaining team.

Before that I was Head of Product at ImmoScout24, Germany's leading property platform, based in Berlin. In 18 months I scaled mobile apps to market dominance across Germany, Austria, and Italy, and led product, design, research, and marketing for the New Homes business. Delivered double-digit revenue growth whilst building cross-functional teams in a genuinely fast-moving scale-up environment.

Earlier in my career I led mobile product strategy at REA Group — realestate.com.au — during the fiercely competitive period with Domain. And I started out at Sensis during the disruption of online directories by Google Maps, which taught me what happens when you don't move fast enough.

My specialisation is Series B–D companies (50–500 employees) in regulated industries — PropTech, HealthTech, Marketplaces — where complexity and growth collide. I'm based in Melbourne and available for in-person work across Australia and remotely worldwide.
